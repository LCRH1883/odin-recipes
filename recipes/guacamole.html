Guacamole

Description

Guacamole is a dip made from avocados, lime juice, and spices. It is often served with tortilla chips or used as a topping for tacos and burritos.

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt.
  2. Mix in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic.
  3. Refrigerate 1 hour for best flavor, or serve immediately.
